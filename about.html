Who We Are

St. Anthony the Great Orthodox Christian Church is a Pan-Orthodox Church that continues the same religious traditions and teachings of the Apostles as learned from Jesus Christ - love God and love your neighbor.

St. Anthony the Great began as an outreach program in 1982 extending from our mother parish, St. George who wanted to establish an Orthodox Christian Church in the Northwest Houston area. Initial meetings were held in a subdivision clubhouse and homes until a temporary place of worship was established in a shopping center suite on FM 1960. Worship then moved to a small property in 1987 after land was purchased by parishioners. In April of 2011, St. Anthony the Great moved to its current location and continues to seek ways to connect with the surrounding community.

We invite you to come and see who we are and have food and fun at our 1st Annual International Food Festival.

International Food Festival

Our International Food Festival is a joyful community celebration with delicious cuisine, warm hospitality, and fun for all ages. Come experience the rich flavors and traditions shared by our parish family while supporting the ongoing ministries of St. Anthony the Great.

Whether you are visiting for your favorite dish, exploring something new, or simply spending time with friends and family, this festival is a beautiful way to connect with the community and enjoy a weekend full of culture, faith, and fellowship.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is parking free?

Yes! Free parking is available on-site at the main church parking lot. Handicap accessible spaces near entrance.

Can I bring my dog?

Service animals are permitted. Please leave pets at home due to food service areas and large crowds.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay.

Are there vegetarian options?

Absolutely! Many Orthodox fasting dishes are naturally vegan. Look for (V) markers on menu items.

Is the festival rain or shine?

Yes! Most vendors are under tents, and the church hall provides indoor space for some activities.

Is there a Lost and Found?

Please email info@satginternationalfoodfest.org for any lost and found items.

Is the festival indoors or outdoors?

The festival includes both indoor and outdoor activities. Church tours will take place inside the church. Tents will be available outdoors for shade.

What are the festival hours?

Friday: 4 PM - 10 PM and Saturday: 11 AM - 10 PM

Is the festival family-friendly?

Yes! Bring the whole family and enjoy a variety of fun activities for the kids at our Children's Village.

When are church tours available?

Church tours will be available on Saturday.
